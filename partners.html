YOUR DONATION makes a difference. HOW?

If you feel reciprocated with one or several of our projects, you might contribute by helping us develop them to their greatest potential.
Projects-focused, we put our intention to action which stands upon humanitarian basics and core motivation - to help Ukrainian people to stand for their Independence, Souverenity, and National unbreakable position.
All collected funds are invested in supplying the most needed material to our local partner Foundations and NGOs in Ukraine. Currently, we collect funds for electric equipment and medicine to be delivered to Ukraine with a second humanitarian aid truck (you can read how we sent our first truck here: https://www.usi.ch/en/feeds/24454).
Thank you for being with us.
We all benefit from being here and now for each other.
